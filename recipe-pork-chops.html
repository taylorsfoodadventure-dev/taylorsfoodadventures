Parmesan Crusted Pork Chops

Taylor's Version — Extra Garlic & Extra Parmesan 💜

🍽️ Serves 4
⚡ Air Fryer
🥩 1-inch Boneless Chops
⏱️ 12–15 Minutes
🛒 Ingredients
  • ½ cup Italian Panko breadcrumbs
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • ½ tsp paprika (regular or smoked)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or beaten egg (for binding)
  • 4 boneless pork chops, 1-inch thick
  • 1 pinch brown sugar optional
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes optional
  • 1 pinch mustard powder optional
👩‍🍳 Steps
1
Dry the chops: Pat pork chops completely dry with paper towels — this is key for a crispy crust.
2
Mix the coating: Combine Panko, Parmesan, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper in a large zip bag or shallow dish. Add any optionals if using.
3
Coat the chops: Lightly brush or rub each chop with olive oil or beaten egg, then press the coating firmly onto all sides.
4
Prep the air fryer: Spray the basket with cooking spray so the coating does not stick. Spray the tops of the coated chops too — this gives extra browning.
5
Arrange chops: Place chops in a single layer. Do not overlap — cook in batches if needed. Air circulation is what makes it crispy!
6
Air fry: Cook at 400°F for 12–15 minutes, flipping halfway through.
7
Check temp and rest: Pork is done at 145°F internal temperature. Let rest 2–3 minutes before serving.
💜 Tips from Taylor
  • Don't overcrowd the basket — air circulation is what makes it crispy!
  • Thicker chops (over 1 inch) may need 2–3 extra minutes.
  • Keep an eye on it the first time — every air fryer runs a little differently.
  • Serve with warm marinara for a chicken parm-style experience!
  • Make a big batch of the dry mix and store in a jar — it keeps for weeks.
