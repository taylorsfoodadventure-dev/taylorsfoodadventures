Parmesan Garlic Breaded Coating Mix

Homemade Shake N' Bake — Extra Garlic, Extra Parm, Always 💜

🧄 Garlic Parm Flavor
🫙 Makes a Big Batch
⏱️ 5 Minutes to Mix
🥩 Use on Anything!
← Back to Recipes
🛒 Ingredients
  • 1 cup Italian Panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1½ tbsp garlic powder
  • 1½ tsp onion powder
  • ½ tsp paprika (regular or smoked)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 1 pinch mustard powder
👩‍🍳 How to Make the Mix
1
Combine everything: Add all ingredients into a large zip-lock bag or a wide shallow bowl. Mix or shake well until everything is evenly combined.
2
Store it: Transfer to an airtight jar or container. Label it and keep it in the pantry. It stays fresh for several weeks!
3
Use it: Whenever you want crispy garlic parm anything — just coat your meat in olive oil or beaten egg, then shake it in the mix and cook!
🍽️ What to Use It On
🥩
Pork chops — Taylor's #1 favorite! Coat and air fry at 400°F for 12–15 min.
🍗
Chicken — Wings, breasts, tenders — garlic parm makes everything better.
🍟
French fries — Toss fries in a little oil, shake in the mix, bake or air fry.
🐟
Fish — Works great on flounder, catfish, or any mild white fish.
💜 Tips from Taylor
  • Make a big batch and store it in a jar — it saves so much time on weeknights!
  • The secret is the Parmesan — it melts into the crust and gives it that golden color.
  • Pat your meat dry before coating — this is the key to getting it really crispy.
  • Spray the tops with cooking spray before air frying for extra browning.
  • This mix is what makes Taylor's Parmesan Pork Chops so good — it's the base recipe!
