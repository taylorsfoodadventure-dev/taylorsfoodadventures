Overcoming Challenges and Embracing Change
April 2026 · Written by Taylor

When I was younger, I often felt stuck in a rut. Life seemed repetitive and I struggled to break free from my routine. However, as I've grown older, things have changed significantly. The turning point came when my mom underwent surgery. During that period, I started staying with Tim for a while, which brought new experiences and responsibilities into my life.

Learning Independence

Living with Tim taught me the importance of self-sufficiency. I began doing chores around the house without being prompted, showing initiative and responsibility. I also learned to cook for myself, which was a big step toward becoming more independent. These experiences have helped me gain confidence and develop new skills.

Bravery in Trying New Foods

This journey is what makes my story interesting. It's all about my bravery — especially when it comes to trying new foods and embracing new challenges. Through these experiences, I've learned to appreciate my own growth and recognize how awesome it feels to step out of my comfort zone.

And basically, I have changed into an independent young woman who has autism and doesn't do picky eating much anymore!

— Taylor 💜
