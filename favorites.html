🍦
Yogurt
Boston Cream Pie and Key Lime are my favorites — they taste like dessert but they're a snack!
🥩
Garlic Parm Pork
Garlic + parmesan + pork = perfection. This is basically my superpower flavor combination.
🍔
Cheeseburger
A classic! Juicy, cheesy, and satisfying — cheeseburgers never miss.
🍕
Pizza
Is there anyone who doesn't love pizza? The answer is no. Pizza is always the right choice.

Taylor's go-to breakfast! Simple, sweet, and perfect to start the day.

One of the best food adventure discoveries! The tapioca pearls are so satisfying.

Always yes. Any flavor. Any time. Ice cream is non-negotiable. 😄

Pudding
