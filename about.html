👧 Who Is Taylor?

13 years old. Proud nerd. Food explorer. Autism advocate. And yes — she knows every movie logo you can think of. 💜

💜 Hey, I'm Taylor!

I'm 13 years old and I have autism. For a long time, I only ate a really small number of foods. That's really common for kids with autism — it's not stubbornness, it's just how our brains work. Textures, smells, and new things can feel overwhelming in a way that's hard to explain.

But things have been changing! I've been staying with my friend Tim and we cook together all the time. I keep a journal of every new food I try — what I loved, what was just okay, and what offended my ancestors. 😂

So far I've tried 73 foods and I'm nowhere near done. Every new try is a win, even if it ends up in the ancestors column!

💌 My message to other kids with autism
"There's nothing wrong with trying new foods — and it's totally okay if you don't like them. The trying is what matters. You don't have to love everything. You just have to be brave enough to take the bite."
— Taylor 💜

🤓 The Nerd Report

Let's be clear — Taylor is not just kind of a nerd. She is an all-time, full-commitment, deep-dive nerd who is in basically every fandom you can name.
