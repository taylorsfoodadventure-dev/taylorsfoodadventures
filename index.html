📖 Taylor's story

Hey! I'm Taylor. I have autism, and for a long time I only ate a small handful of foods. A lot of kids with autism are like that — it's not being picky, it's just how our brains work with textures, smells, and new things.

Since I started staying a lot with my friend Tim, we've been cooking together and trying new foods every week. I keep a journal of everything I try — what I loved, what was just okay, and what offended my ancestors. 😂

So far I've tried 79 foods and I'm not stopping! I even helped break down a whole pork loin — I cut about a quarter of it and ran it through the cuber attachment myself.

This site is for me — but it's also for every kid with autism who is still working on their food journey. You've got this. 💜
